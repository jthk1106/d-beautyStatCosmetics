Question:How long does Universal C Skin Refiner last on my skin?

Answer:
Universal C Skin Refiner contains 20% pure vitamin C in a time released deliver system. It will continually release vitamin C for eight to twelve hours. After application the product is absorbed by the skin, so you don’t have to worry about removing it, when you cleanse or reapply more product. For best / quickest results apply twice a day. If you prefer to apply just once a day, no problem it will just take a little longer to see long term results.

Question:Can I use Universal C with Retin-A?

Answer:
Yes, apply Retin-A first following with Universal C. You may find your need for using Retin-A to diminish over time due to the skin perfecting effects of Universal C.

Question:My skin is sensitive, is Universal C too strong for me?

Answer:
20% is a very potent amount of vitamin C in its most active state, L-ascorbic acid. It is also activated for maximum absorption and performance by the tartaric acid, a double strength alpha hydroxyl acid. Also present is the powerful ant-inflammatory active of green tea EGCG, at a significantly high level, not possible except in our patented formula. This should keep most skin, even sensitive able to use and see results quickly without any adverse effects.

Question:Do your products contain Silicone?

Answer:
Yes. Silicone is one of the most valued ingredients used in luxury skin care. Derived from sand, it undergoes extensive processing before being added to beauty products. Providing exceptional sensorial aesthetics such as smoothness of application, spreadability, wrinkle reduction, line filling and producing a soft focus younger finish are some of the unique benefits of Silicones. Silicones also create a moisturizing film that aids in skin hydration yet controls oil. Additionally silicone exhibits excellent non tacky, non- oily, smoothing characteristics. Silicones large molecule size keeps them on the surface of the skin. The films are breathable therefore they are very compatible. Silicones used in our formulas have been extensively tested for safety including non-irritating, non-allergic, non-acnegenic, non-mutagenic, and non-estrogenic.

Question:What should I do if my skin feels dry after using Universal C?

Answer:
While Universal C does not dry the skin, is not a moisturizer. It is a powerful skin refining treatment. Depending upon the time of the year or your skin type, a nourishing moisturizer may be necessary to feel more comfortable. We recommend using Universal Pro-Bio Moisture Boost Cream, after each application.

Question:What should I do if my skin tingles after applying BeautyStat Universal C Skin Refiner?

Answer: 
It is possible that people may experience a tingling sensation when using powerful and active 20% Vitamin C.
Usually this dissipates after a few minutes. If not, cut back usage to once a day, and follow with Universal Pro-Bio Moisture Boost Cream.

Question:What should I do if I experience other negative reactions such as redness, irritation, burning?

Answer: 
Our products are extensively tested for safety. However, some people may still experience a negative reaction. Cut your usage down to once a day and always follow with Universal Pro-Bio Moisture Boost Cream.

Question:May I use another moisturizer?

Answer: 
It’s possible that other moisturizers may help, because of their moisturizing ingredients. However, we may only endorse Universal Pro-Bio Moisture Boost Cream as the two products have been tested together and show synergy and perfect compatibility. If a severe reaction occurs, stop using immediately and call your dermatologist if necessary.
